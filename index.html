Meet the founders

Laplace College Consulting was built by students who navigated the process at the highest level — and then turned that experience into a repeatable system for others.

Neal Karani was admitted to Stanford from Lambert High School and has been helping students get into top colleges ever since — with a style that’s structured, calm, and honest about tradeoffs. Cofounder Arhan Barve was admitted to Harvard from Lambert High School and also received a Stanford likely letter; he brings deep pattern recognition across activities, positioning, and application strategy.

Together, they focus on the parts that matter: building a coherent profile over time and translating it into applications that read sharp, specific, and real.

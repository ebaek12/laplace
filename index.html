How it works

Our process defines the work for each month so families know what needs to happen next.

  1. 01

    Diagnose

    We review your academic record, current activities, and college goals to identify the most important gaps.

  2. 02

    Build roadmap

    We build a plan for courses, testing, extracurricular activities, and application deadlines.

  3. 03

    Execute weekly

    Each week you receive concrete tasks and feedback on completed work.

  4. 04

    Essays and positioning

    We convert your work into strong activity entries and essay drafts.

  5. 05

    Submit strong

    Before submission, we complete final quality checks and deadline verification.
